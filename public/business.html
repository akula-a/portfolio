Innovation Scholars

Member

The Innovation Scholars program fosters innovation and entrepreneurship and seeks to increase the number of successful startup companies initiated by or involving Carnegie Mellon University’s undergraduate students. Scholars complete a two-year program integrating academic coursework, work experience in a startup company, networking, mentorship — given as well as received.
